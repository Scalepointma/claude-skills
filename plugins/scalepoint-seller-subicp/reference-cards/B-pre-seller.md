# Sub-ICP B · Pre-Seller / Exploring

> **Internal label.** Plain enough to say internally but doesn't need to surface to the seller.

## Essence

Selling in 1–3 years. Doing my homework now.

Owner who has decided to sell but is not ready to list today. The opposite of A — commitment without readiness.

## Telltale signs

- Talks in 18–36 month horizons; can name a target year or season
- Has met with accountant about books cleanup, or a lawyer about structure
- Asks process questions ("how does a CIM work?", "how long does selling usually take?")
- Has a future triggering event (retirement age, lease end, kid finishing school)
- Engages with prep tools (Exit Readiness, valuation calculator) rather than buyer-search tools

## Easy to confuse with

- **A (Valuation-Curious).** Distinguishing question: *"In the last 90 days, have you talked to an accountant, lawyer, or family about selling?"* B has. A hasn't.
- **C (Committed Main Street).** Distinguishing question: *"If a qualified buyer walked in tomorrow with a fair offer, would you take it?"* B says "I'd consider, but I want to be properly prepared first." C says "yes, if the number is right."

## Match Matrix verdicts for row B

| Buyer cohort | Verdict |
|---|---|
| Strategic Acquirer | Weak |
| Searcher (ETA) | Possible |
| Owner-Operator | **Strong** |
| LP / Passive | Weak |
| Family Office | Weak |
| Holdco / Roll-up | Weak |
| Searcher-Broker | Possible |

**Owner-Operator is the only Strong match.** Owner-Operator buyers are patient and prep-friendly — they're the one cohort that can actually wait for a B seller to be ready. Searcher and Searcher-Broker are Possible (their timelines may or may not align). Funds (LP, Family Office, Holdco) are too early for them. Strategic is Weak — they want a deal, not a runway.

## First-meeting move

Engagement Letter is **premature**.

- Stay in nurture with a real 90-day re-check on the calendar
- Sell coaching or a premium widget tier if they're hungry to move things along
- Capture target year explicitly so the system can re-surface them at the right time
- Optionally start light buyer-side conversations with patient Owner-Operator buyers — not formal intros, just "I'm starting to look in 18 months"

## What changes B → something else

- B's life accelerates (health, partner pushing out) → E
- B's books get cleaned up and they decide to move now → C or D depending on revenue
- B drifts and stops engaging → back to A or archive
